Water Resources and Engineering

Most my work and research experience after graduation as an engineer has ocourred as a Water Resources Engineer in DF+ Engenharia since early 2019.

At DF+, I have worked in several infrastructure design projects and studies for the mining industries, mostly for Vale at Brucutu & Agua Limpa, Itabira and Vargem Grande mining complexes. Ranging from pre-conceptual to executive levels, my main area of focus has been on operation and drainage of shared tailing and waste dumps. I have also acted on studies of Dam safety and design, rainfall frequency analysis, PMP computation, hydraulic works design, reservoir operation simulation and water quality studies, with ocasional participation on dam-break studies.
